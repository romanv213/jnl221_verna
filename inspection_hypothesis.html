An interesting hypothesis I have for this dataset is: For restaurants that have been re-inspected, the total number of critical violations is lower upon the next re-inspections, compared the the original inspection. This data comes from the Onondaga County Health Department's Restaurant Inspections Database. I think this is a good hypothesis because it allows us to see if the system of restaurant inspections encourages foodservice facilities to cut down on their violations, or if they continue unchanged.
