Syracuse University, Fall 2025

This dataviz plots out the wards in Syracuse with the highest percentage of poor or very poor quality roads. This is part of a larger story on what specific neighborhoods have undermaintained roads.

After making this chart, I got some very good feedback from my partner. One, I misidentified my finding in the data- the sixth ward has the highest percentage, not the fourth. Two, my subheading was quite short. Lastly, I didn't have a link to the original data.

I first changed my heading to reflect the accurate data finding. Next, I reworked my subheading to be a little more descriptive so it can stand alone. Lastly, I added a link and attribution to the SMTC and Open Data Syracuse.
